About Me

Get to know who I am!

Growing up in Utah, I have gained a deep love of the outdoors! I love to hike, mountain bike, rock climb, swim, camp, and rappel.

I love animals, and I am a huge dog person. I currently have two dogs, a labrador and mini goldendoodle, and a calico cat!

I love to play and listen to music. I sing and play the piano, guitar, and ukulele. I have been taking piano lessons for 15 years!
